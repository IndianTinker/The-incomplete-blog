My name is Rohit Gupta.I am a student of Electrical Engineering from Delhi Technological University( formerly Delhi College Of Engineering), Delhi,India.I have a keen interest in Effective Energy Utilization , Electric Mobility,Hybrid and Fuel Cell Vehicles,LEDs and Technology in General. I am an experimental learner (the reason why i have a pretty hard time “learning” at college :D).

I love to tinker with stuff ,make them cooler and efficient. I advocate OpenSource Learning because i believe in the fact that “everyone learns stuff from the internet and hence we must always contribute to the same and not be a “dead-node”.”

I am a member of my college`s SEV(Solar Electric Vehicle) Team-Solaris . I love making vehicles that demonstrate potential use of energy to make our mobility future proof and greener. You can refer to the articles on the blog regarding this solar car project of ours. I have been fortunate enough to be a part of the team that has made 2 successful solar vehicles – Avenir (WSC`11) and Arka. We are working on a 3rd one as well.
